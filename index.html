Why Majibu

Three principles.
One coherent strategy.

Leverage

Your philanthropic capital is amplified through a curated portfolio of organisations with proven track records, strong local relationships, and the capacity to scale. We ensure every pound works harder.

Localisation

We back organisations that are rooted in their communities — staffed, led, and owned locally. This isn't a principle we compromise on. Local solutions are more effective, more sustainable, and more dignifying.

Legacy

Multi-year funding horizons allow partners to plan, grow, and embed change that outlasts individual projects. We're building institutions, not running programmes.

Majibu gave us the confidence and the capital to build the systems we always knew were possible. For the first time, we could plan beyond the next grant cycle.

Where we focus

Impact areas

Our partners work across six interconnected domains where local leadership delivers the greatest long-term returns.

Education & Youth

Supporting access to quality learning and skills development for young people in underserved communities.

Health & Wellbeing

Strengthening community health systems and increasing access to essential services.

Livelihoods & Enterprise

Building economic resilience through vocational training, microenterprise support, and market access.

Environment & Conservation

Working with coastal and inland communities to protect natural resources and build climate resilience.

Child Protection

Reducing vulnerability, preventing exploitation, and strengthening family and community safeguarding systems.

Digital Inclusion

Expanding access to technology and digital literacy to unlock opportunity across all other sectors.
