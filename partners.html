How we select

The partnership process

We don't issue open calls for applications. Our partnerships begin with relationships — direct referrals from people who know both the organisation and what Majibu stands for.

1

Introduction & dialogue

We begin with open conversations to understand an organisation's context, values, leadership, and vision — not a formal application.

2

Due diligence

A thorough but proportionate review of governance, financials, programme evidence, and community relationships — conducted with transparency on both sides.

3

Multi-year agreement

If there's genuine mutual fit, we formalise a multi-year partnership with clear expectations, reporting requirements, and technical support commitments.
