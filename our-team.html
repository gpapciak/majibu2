The people behind Majibu

Our team

A small, expert team combining decades of East Africa NGO experience with specialist expertise in finance, governance, data, and philanthropy.

JW

Joanna Waddington MBE

Director & Founder

Joanna has spent more than 25 years working in and around civil society in East Africa, holding senior leadership roles across programme management, organisational development, and philanthropic strategy. She founded Majibu from a deeply held conviction that the most effective change in the region comes from local organisations with the right resources and the right partners behind them.

Awarded an MBE for services to international development, Joanna brings an unmatched combination of field credibility and UK philanthropic network to Majibu's work. She leads all major donor relationships and has final oversight of the partnership portfolio.

KW

Kennedy Wanyoni

Digital Data Analysis

Kennedy leads Majibu's approach to data systems and digital monitoring across the partner portfolio. He brings technical expertise in data analysis, digital tools for programme management, and building accessible M&E frameworks that genuinely serve local organisations.

WH

William Harnden

Financial Sustainability

William works with Majibu's partner organisations on long-term financial planning, income diversification, and building the financial management systems they need to operate with confidence and transparency.

GP

Greg Papciak

Governance & M&E

Greg specialises in governance structures, board development, and monitoring and evaluation frameworks. He helps Majibu partners build the institutional foundations needed to attract multi-year funding and deliver measurable outcomes.

HF

Helen Fairclough

Philanthropy Advisor

Helen draws on extensive experience in high-net-worth philanthropy to support Majibu's donor relationships. She helps philanthropists think through their strategic giving objectives and ensures their engagement with Majibu is meaningful and well-structured.

“
Our team is deliberately small. Every person here has been chosen because they bring something irreplaceable — deep relationships, rare expertise, or both. We don't do things at scale for its own sake.
Joanna Waddington MBE — Director, Majibu
Beyond the core team

A wider network of expertise

The Majibu core team is supported by a trusted network of specialists across legal, communications, programme design, and regional expertise. We draw on this network to provide targeted technical support to partners — ensuring they get the specific expertise they need, when they need it.

This model keeps Majibu lean while ensuring our partners are never short of the right expertise.
